Media And Entertainment Apps

In this era, Media & Entertainment (M & E) businesses are more often relying on AI solutions to meet the immense increment in media consumption. This is the digital revolution. The M & E industry comprises six segments namely; Film & TV, Social Media, Journalism, Music, Gaming, and Sports & Gambling. In the film industry, AI helps to sort out whether an actor is favorable for casting or not, based on previous records or fan following, can also anticipate the success rate of any film. AI can be used to formulate a piece of new music from old or a mixture of music.

Fashion Industry

In the fashion industry, before purchasing any product virtually trying it increases the confidence of the person to purchase it. For example, the Nail try- on gives the confidence to customers to virtually try nail paints. It includes nail arts along with plain nail colors. It gives the real effect to users. The advisory role of AI is admirable in the fashion industry. AI can recommend clothes, shoes, and accessories depending on a person's real-time features and statics. AI is being used tremendously for forecasting the clothing trends (men and women) to save the stock from getting wasted by not getting sold.

OCR

All the information printed on the documents can be extracted automatically and further used for the digitization of systems. It was not the case in earlier times; people used to manually write all the data for digitization which took a lot more time for the process. The types of documents which can be dealt with are receipts, medical reports, examination test papers, license documents, resumes, AutoCad measurements, and many more.

Surveillance

Surveillance of places, people, and things is primarily done using AI these days. Suspicious events or persons behaving oddly can be detected by tracking and video processing on run-time videos. Customers which used conventional household door locking systems now use face recognition systems and object detection using different algorithms to provide remote surveillance of houses to customers. Different countries are investing too much in the field of surveillance. Heavy deployment of cameras is being done. Camera monitoring is required 24/7.
